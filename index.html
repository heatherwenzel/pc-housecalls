Computers can be frustrating.

You don't have to set up or fix your computer on your own. PC Housecall of Grand Rapids will troubleshoot, diagnose and fix your computer for you. I'll come to you or even connect remotely to your computer, no matter what your need is.

Services

Virus/malware removal

I'll find and remove any viruses or malware, make sure your computer has all updates/patches applied, and ensure antivirus and antimalware are installed.

New PC setup

I'll come to you and unbox, set up and connect your new PC to your printer and internet. I'll also install any software or programs you've purchased or downloaded.

Tune-up

Is something wrong but you're not sure what? Is your computer running slow but you don't know why? I'll scan, diagnose and fix your computer based on your individual needs.
